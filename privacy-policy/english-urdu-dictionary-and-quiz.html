Privacy Policy for English Urdu Dictionary & Quiz

Effective Date: April 14, 2026

We operate the English Urdu Dictionary & Quiz mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store personal data on our servers.

All data such as saved words, quiz progress, and preferences is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to us.

However, third-party services (such as Google Analytics and Google AdMob) may collect anonymized data including:

  • Device information
  • App usage data
  • Advertising identifiers

How We Use Information

  • Improve app functionality
  • Analyze user behavior
  • Display advertisements

Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data on external servers.

All user data (such as saved words or quiz results) is stored locally on your device and remains there until you delete it manually or uninstall the application.

Third-party services may retain anonymized data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Deletion

You have full control over your data.

  • Clear app data from app profile section by clicking reset all progress button.
  • Uninstall the app

We do not have access to your locally stored data and cannot delete it remotely.

Permissions Usage

Our app uses certain permissions only to support essential functionality. The RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED permission allows the app to automatically restore important services, such as reminders or scheduled alerts, after your device restarts, ensuring uninterrupted operation. The POST_NOTIFICATIONS permission enables us to send relevant notifications like reminders, alerts, or updates, which you can control or disable anytime through your device settings. The SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM permission is used to trigger precise, time-sensitive events such as alarms or scheduled notifications that must occur at exact times. These permissions are strictly used for app functionality, and we do not collect, store, or share any personal data through them.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized usage data. This helps us improve app performance and user experience.

Advertising

Our app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect anonymized data to show relevant advertisements.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
