Privacy Policy for English Urdu Translator

Effective Date: March 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the English Urdu Translator mobile application developed by Nova Utility Labs. This policy explains how we handle user information when you use our app.

Information Collection and Use

The app does not collect or store any personal user data. All translations (text, voice, and documents) are processed securely and are not stored or shared by the developer.

Permissions Used

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Microphone: To enable voice translation
  • Camera: To capture images for translation (OCR)
  • Storage / Files: To access PDF and Word documents for translation
  • Internet: Required to process translations

These permissions are used strictly for app functionality and not for collecting personal data.

Data Security

User data processed within the app is encrypted and handled securely. We do not store or retain any personal or sensitive user information.
